اختر القسم

ما القسم الذي يوجد فيه حل لمشكلتك؟

Orthopedic

Deals with the musculoskeletal system including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles

Respirology

Deals with diagnosis and treats diseases of the respiratory system , the lungs and other organs that help you breathe

Cardiology

Cardiology deal with diagnosis and treats patients with diseases and defects of the heart and blood vessels

Dentistry

Dealing with the prevention and treatment of diseases of the teeth, gums, and oral cavity

Ophthalmology

Deal with the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders and vision care

Radiology

Deal with diagnosis and treats injuries and diseases using medical imaging
